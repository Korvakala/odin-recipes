Oatmeal

Description

Traditional finnish oatmeal is a core memory for most of the children there. Nothing tastes more dull in itself, makes the parents so happy by being so cheap and is so healthy at the same time.

Prep Time: 0 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Additional Time: 0 mins

Total Time: 15 min

Servings: 2

With just a handful of stable ingredients, you can also eat oatmeal and gather those generational traumas!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of oats
  • 2 cup of water (twice the amount of oats
  • ½ teaspoon of salt

Steps

Follow these steps to prepare your oatmeal

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Put all the ingredients into the pot on the stove
  3. Turn heat to about ½ of maximum and wait until it boils
  4. When boiling, turn the heat to minimum
  5. Stir occasionally
  6. After 10 to 15 minutes of boiling, check if ready
  7. When ready, serve and eat immediately
